Instructions:

  • To draw, click the DRAW button. After that, click to create 4 points anywhere on canvas. Program will then draw a Bezier curve with the chosen color in the color selector above.

  • To edit, click the EDIT button. After that, slowly use click and drag on any existing curve's points.

  • To delete, click the DELETE button. After that, click on desired curve's approximated vertices.

  • To change curve color, first click on the color selector on top of the page and select a color, then click the CHANGE COLOR button (under line tools). After that, click on the desired curve's approximated vertices.

  • CLEAR button wipes the canvas.

  • To change background color, first click on the color selector on top of the page and select a color, then click the CHANGE COLOR button (under canvas tools).

Have fun!
